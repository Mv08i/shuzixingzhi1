Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is the website completely free?
A: Yes, Number Properties Lookup is completely free to use. You don't need to pay any fees.
Q: What types of numbers are supported?
A: We support positive integers, negative integers, positive decimals, negative decimals, and zero. Each type will display the corresponding mathematical properties.
Q: Can I modify the GCD comparison number?
A: Yes, there is an input field for "GCD comparison number" next to the main input. You can customize the number to compare (default is 100).
Q: How does prime factorization work for decimals?
A: Decimals are converted to fractional form, then the numerator and denominator are prime factorized separately. For example, 0.5 is converted to 1/2.
Q: How can I provide improvement suggestions?
A: You can contact us via email and let us know what features you'd like to add or improve.
Q: Is my data stored?
A: No data is stored. We don't store any numbers you input. All calculations are performed locally.
